Pak court rejects plea to drop terrorism charges against Pervez Musharraf

ISLAMABAD: A Pakistani court has dismissed a petition seeking deletion of charges against former military dictator and transfer of his case from the Anti-Terrorism Court (ATC) to the sessions court, according to a media report.


An Islamabad high court division bench comprising Chief Justice Athar Minallah and Justice Miangul Hassan Aurangzeb resumed the hearing of the petition filed by 75-year-old Musharraf through his lawyer Akhtar Shah.
